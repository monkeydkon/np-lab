About Us Who are we?

We are a team that consists of four young, creative and ambitious people. We all study computer science and share the same passion, solving problems. This project is the result after months of dreaming, evolving, learning new exciting things about the world of technology until we felt ready to start working with other people. That time has come. The Net Programming Lab is here for you. We constantly keep track of new technologies. We bring you the most modern web technologies of our time. Don't be afraid. You trust us to help you. It's a deal.

How we work

Analysis & Specification

First of all, we talk to our clients, trying to really understand what needs to be done. Then we discuss and find the most efficient way of acting.

Blueprint & design

But we never just start with coding. Before that, we visualise the project so that we make sure you get what you want. And that what you want is beautiful.

Development

Now is the time for the actual making of your website. As a team of four people, we split the project into parts, making it less and less dificcult to make mistakes.

Launch

Finally, after your website is all ready, you can be sure that we will do all the necessary for your website to be online. You leave it all to us.

Our Services What we do?

Web design & development

We make beautiful, modern, responsive websites. Do you want to advertise yourself or your company? Do you want to do it the modern way? We are what you are looking for. This is what we do. Having years of learning, we can provide you with the best of services. The web is our home and we strive to make it even better.

Wordpress development

We guess that people want flexible websites that can be handled by their owners. This is the reason we use Wordpress. Whether it is a blog that you want, or a forum, Wordpress is the choice. It's simple and clean. You don't have to mess with any of the code.

E-commerce

Even if it is products you want to sell, we also have the solution. Do you want to expand your business worldwide? We can help you get started by developing your online functional E-Shop. The future of your company is now in good hands. Sky is the limit.

Graphic & logo design

In our days, good aesthetics is essential. Let us carry on that weight too. Your team or company or even yourself can become much more appealing with the help of a nice looking logo. Whether it is a simple one, or a more complex. It is all about the beauty. You have to stick into the minds of the people that you are the best just by the first look. This is the only way you can look different in the internet.
